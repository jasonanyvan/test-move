Navigation

Navigation bar

A navigation rails provide access to primary destinations in apps when using tablet and desktop screens

The navigation rail is a side navigation component that displays between 3 to 7 links. Each destination is represented by an icon and label text.

The rail can serve as the sole navigation component on larger screen sizes, such as desktop and tablet.

When your product is used on small screens, the navigation rail should be swapped for a navigation bar. When your product is used on desktop screens, the navigation rail can be swapped for a navigation drawer.

The navigation rail is placed on the leading edge of the screen; on the left side for left-to-right languages, and on the right side for right-to-left languages.
