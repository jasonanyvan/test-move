App bars

App bars provide content and display information, and actions, at the top of a screen

These bars should primarily be used in the adminstrative space and to a lesser extent on our marketing efforts. To reduce the amount of elements on a screen, app bars should always replace the main navigation. This applies primarily to mobile, as desktop can have both navigation rails or expanded navigation drawers

Mobile

There are two types of mobile app bars â€” compact and stacked

Compact app bars allow for a single quick action and a short page title

Stacked app bars allow for multiple quick actions and longer page titles

Desktop

Provides a quick action to return to the previous screen, without having to click on an item in a navigation rail or expanded navigation drawer

Toggles the navigation drawer to reduce the amount of items on the screen

The primary use case for the app bar on desktop can be found on the administrative side of the system - using the entire bar as a search mechanism
