Banners

A banner is a persistent static container which is sometimes closable by user actions

A banner is a one-line, full color, full width container that can be used to communicate short snippets of information to users. Banners are un-intrusive, non-dismissible, and only support link buttons

Global banners

These are used for critical issues or messages that should be conveyed with prominence. Usually, they are placed on top of a page above everything else or above the global navigation bar.

Sectional banners

These can be placed on a particular area of a page or top of a component they correspond to. These messages may contain a variety of content types including errors, information etc.
